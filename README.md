# SnapSplash

A first-person underwater adventure game where you play as Scuba Dude, exploring the depths of a tropical ocean to photograph beautiful sea creatures. Dive deep, capture stunning photos, sell them for profit, and upgrade your gear to explore even deeper waters!

## üåä About the Game

Welcome to SnapSplash Island, home to the world's two best hobbies: scuba diving and photographing fish! As Scuba Dude, embark on an underwater photography adventure across three distinct ocean depths. Take pictures of exotic sea creatures, sell them to the friendly Pirate Shopkeeper, and use your earnings to purchase better scuba equipment that allows you to venture into the mysterious deep ocean.

**Core Features:**
- First-person underwater exploration with realistic swimming mechanics
- Camera system with flash mechanics to stun enemies
- Three distinct ocean levels (Shallow, Medium, Deep) each with unique challenges
- Dynamic shop system with multiple upgrades
- Enemy encounters including sharks and a mysterious giant squid
- Oxygen management and depth pressure systems

## üê† Gameplay

**Objective:** Explore the ocean depths, photograph fish, and survive long enough to discover what lurks at the bottom.

**Controls:**
- **WASD** - Move/Swim
- **SPACE** - Jump (on land)
- **Mouse** - Look around
- **Right-Click (Hold)** - Equip camera
- **Left-Click** - Take photo
- **E** - Interact with anchors and shopkeeper
- **F** - Toggle flashlight (after purchase)

**Game Loop:**
1. Use the anchor to dive into the ocean
2. Take pictures of fish while managing your air supply
3. Return to the anchor before running out of air
4. Sell your photos to the Pirate Shopkeeper
5. Purchase upgrades to explore deeper levels
6. Discover new species and face dangerous creatures

## üéØ Ocean Levels

### Shallow Ocean
Your starting point with friendly fish and calm waters. Perfect for beginners to learn the photography mechanics.

### Middle Ocean
Venture deeper to find rarer fish, but beware of the **Shark**! This predator will chase you and drain your oxygen if you get too close. Use your camera's flash to stun it and escape.

### Deep Ocean (Final Challenge)
The darkest depths where the legendary **Squid** resides. Photographing this creature triggers an intense chase sequence back to the surface. Survive, and you'll earn enough money to purchase all upgrades!

## üõí Shop Upgrades

Purchase from the Pirate Shopkeeper to enhance your abilities:

- **More Storage** - Take more photos per dive
- **More Air** - Increase underwater time
- **Depth Gauge** - Go deeper without passing out from pressure
- **Scuba Fins** - Swim faster underwater
- **Flashlight** - See clearly in the dark depths

## üé® Game Design

**Story:**
On a tropical island in the Pacific Ocean lives a man with one passion: photographing fish. Armed with his camera and scuba gear, he partners with a local Pirate Shopkeeper who buys his photos and sells him equipment. Together, they fuel each other's fascination with the ocean's mysterious beauty.

**Characters:**
- **Scuba Dude** - Happy-go-lucky underwater photographer with a passion for marine life
- **Pirate Shopkeeper** - Friendly merchant who buys photos and sells scuba equipment
- **Shark** - Aggressive predator that attacks divers in the middle ocean
- **Squid** - Legendary deep-sea creature and final boss
- **Fish** - Various NPCs swimming throughout the ocean depths

### Development Highlights

- Designed photography mechanic with distance-based scoring
